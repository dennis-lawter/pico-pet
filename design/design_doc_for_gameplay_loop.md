# Day to Day
## Working
You can always go to the pomodoro screen, even at night or on weekends.
Here you can start cycles.
Completing 1 work cycle = 4 food.
Completing 1 short rest = 1 food.
Completing 1 long rest = 2 food.

## Feeding
Throughout each day, the hunger meter grows hungrier.
During the night, you cannot access the feed menu.
Through the night, the hunger will not decrease.

On the food menu, show the hunger meter.
Once you do feed, you can press confirm again to +1 the food spent.

## Watering
Watering represents doing your daily "atomic habits".
Maybe that's taking pills, brushing teeth, or just drinking a glass of water.
Every day you must "check in" for these habits by "watering" the garden.

## Sleeping
There's an action to "sleep" the town/pet.
User sets a sleep time.
Show hunger before the user sleeps.
If the user does not perform the sleep action, after 30m, autosleep occurs with happiness penalty.
Whenever the sleep happens, we save the state to NVM.
